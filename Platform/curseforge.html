Vanilla Outsider: Bat Ecology

Some call them pests. We call them the night shift.

Bat Ecology gives the bat a purpose. No longer just ambient noise, bats are now a functioning society with behaviors, cycles, and farming utility.

1. Dynamic Roosting

Bats adhere to a strict day/night cycle. During the day, they find a dark ceiling and sleep (hanging upside down). This isn't just visualâ€”it completely shuts down their AI, saving server performance.

2. Swarm Intelligence

Bats are social. They form colonies with a specific Leader. Where the Leader goes, the pack follows. This creates beautiful, fluid flocking patterns instead of chaotic random movement.

3. Farming Utility

  • Guano: Passive drops while bats fly. Weak fertilizer.
  • Pollination: Use a Lantern to attract a swarm at night. As they orbit the light, they trigger a Pollination Burst, bonemealing crops in a 9x9 radius.
